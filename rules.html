Rules & Regulations

Maintaining discipline for an effective learning environment.

1. Attendance & Punctuality

  • Students must be present in the school premises 10 minutes before the morning assembly.
  • Minimum 75% attendance is required to appear in the final examinations.
  • Leave applications must be submitted in advance by the parents/guardians.

2. Uniform & Appearance

  • Students must wear the prescribed school uniform neatly pressed.
  • Shoes must be polished daily.
  • Personal hygiene and neat haircuts are mandatory.

3. Discipline & Conduct

  • Use of mobile phones or electronic gadgets is strictly prohibited on campus.
  • Any damage to school property will be penalized and charged to the student's parents.
  • Students are expected to behave politely and respectfully with teachers and staff.
